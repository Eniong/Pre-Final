Duties and Responsibilities of Students

Every student shall:

Section 1. Love God, his fellowmen as he loves himself, and strive to lead virtuous and useful life.

Section 2. Observe and uphold at all times the Code of Conduct and Discipline the rules, regulations and policies promulgated by the University.

Section 3. Support and participate actively in the SSC initiated programs, projects and activities; and in promotion if the general welfare and in the attainment of a just, orderly and compassionate society.

Section 4. Help in the exercise of individual and social rights and responsiblities the strengthening of freedom, and enhancement of cooperation among students faculty, staff and administration in the pursuit of a progressive and stable academic environment for national progress.

Section 5. Pay authorized dues, fees and contributions willfully, and protect preserve the properties of the University.

Section 6. Respect the rights of fellow students, the members of the faculty and the administrative staff of the University: the role of faculty as a surrogate parent shall also be recognized and respected by every student.

Section 7. Respect and strive to perpetuate the desirable customs and traditions of the Filipino people, its duly constituted authorities, the laws of the land, and the rules and policies of the University.

Section 8. Uphold the academic and moral integrity if the University by achieving excellence and moral uprightness.

Section 9. Promote and preserve the peace and order, harmony, camaraderie and unity in the University by observing the rules on discipline and harmonious relationship with fellow students and with the University personnel.

Section 10. Exercise his rights responsively in the knowledge that he is answerable to God for any violation of the general welfare and of the rights of others.

Section 11. Defend and protect the Constitution and By-Laws and abide by provisions.
